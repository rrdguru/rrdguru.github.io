Teaching Philosophy

My teaching approach begins with a simple premise: before solving a problem, students must first understand it properly. Rather than focusing on quick answers, my courses encourage careful reasoning, questioning assumptions, and examining problems from multiple perspectives.

Across courses in marketing strategy, product management, branding, and research methods, students engage with managerial problems through case discussions, analytical frameworks, and applied projects. The objective is not only to transmit knowledge but to cultivate structured thinking.
