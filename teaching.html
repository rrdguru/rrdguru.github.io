Thematic Analysis of Student Feedback

A qualitative synthesis of over 5 years of student evaluations reveals three consistent pillars in my teaching methodology:

Data-Driven Rigor

Students consistently highlight the emphasis on quantitative justification. Feedback indicates a strong appreciation for bridging theoretical marketing frameworks with hard analytical tools (R, Python) to solve real business cases.

Industry Relevance

Evaluations frequently mention the "boardroom approach." By leveraging my prior corporate experience at Fact Works and SAIL, students report high satisfaction in learning how concepts apply directly to current market scenarios.

Engaging Pedagogy

Across different cultures—from Geneva to Bodhgaya to Ranchi—feedback points to a highly interactive classroom environment that encourages debate, peer learning, and critical questioning of established norms.
