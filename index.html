Academic Instruction

Teaching Philosophy

I teach marketing and research as fundamental analytical skills rather than isolated subjects. My approach leverages my background in iron and steel plant operations, software engineering, and strategic consulting.

  • Problem First, Framework Second: I require students to interrogate a problem and articulate what is actually being asked before they reach for a solution.
  • Critical Thinking as a Practice: Refusing to let comfortable explanations sit unchallenged.
  • Uncompromising Rigour: The best learning occurs when maximum intellectual rigor is paired with explicitly protected psychological safety.
