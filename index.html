The Foundation

An Unconventional Path

Most marketing academics come through a single door. I came through three. I started as an electrical engineer, worked full-time at SAIL and Infosys, and moved to strategy consulting at Fact Works GmbH in Berlin. Only then did I commit fully to a PhD at the University of Geneva.

That sequence is not accidental. The industry grounding shapes the research questions. The research rigor shapes the teaching. The teaching keeps both honest.

Current Mandate: South Asian University

Serving as founding faculty in the newly established Faculty of Management Studies at an eight-nation SAARC university. Directing the design of programs, governance, and student life from scratch.

  • Teaching Business Research Methods and Analytics.
  • Leading placement strategies and alumni networks.
  • Fostering cross-border business perspectives.
