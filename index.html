Academic Profile

My work lies at the intersection of marketing strategy, consumer behavior and technological innovation. My background combines engineering training and IT industry experience with doctoral research in marketing. This interdisciplinary foundation allows me to integrate analytics, strategy and behavioral insight in both research and teaching.

I have taught diverse student cohorts across Switzerland, Russia and India. Currently I serve as a founding faculty member in the Faculty of Management at South Asian University, contributing to curriculum development and institutional growth in a new international university.

Research Agenda

My research examines how consumers perceive product quality and how these perceptions influence brand relationships, adoption of new technologies and sustainable consumption.

  • Customer experienced product quality
  • Consumer behaviour and brand relationships
  • Electric vehicle adoption and sustainability
  • Analytics driven marketing insights

My current research includes a longitudinal study on consumer experience with electric vehicles, conducted in collaboration with international research partners.
