Academic Profile

I am a marketing academic dedicated to exploring how product quality shapes consumer-brand relationships and drives sustainable consumption. With a Ph.D. from the University of Geneva and over five years of prior industry experience in engineering and technology, my approach bridges rigorous quantitative methodologies with practical business fundamentals.

Currently serving as a Senior Assistant Professor at South Asian University (SAU), I am deeply involved in institution building, academic governance, and doctoral supervision. I advocate for an educational approach where critical thinking and core literature remain central, even as we integrate advanced analytics and AI into the modern business curriculum.
