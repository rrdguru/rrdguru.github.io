Academic Profile

My research examines how consumers experience product quality and how this shapes brand relationships and sustainable consumption. I combine quantitative modelling with managerial insights to understand real world decision making.

My teaching spans institutions across Europe, Russia and India, including the University of Geneva, HSE Moscow, IIM Bodh Gaya and IIM Ranchi. Courses range from branding and marketing strategy to analytics and product management.
