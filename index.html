Academic Profile

My work lies at the intersection of marketing strategy, consumer behaviour and technological innovation. My background combines engineering training and industry experience in IT with doctoral research in marketing. This interdisciplinary perspective enables integration of analytics, behavioural insights and managerial decision frameworks.

I have taught diverse student cohorts across Switzerland, Russia and India. Currently I serve as founding faculty in the Faculty of Management at South Asian University, contributing to curriculum design and institutional development.

Research Agenda

My research examines how consumers perceive product quality and how these perceptions influence brand relationships, technology adoption and sustainable consumption.

  • Customer experienced product quality
  • Consumer behaviour and brand relationships
  • Electric vehicle adoption and sustainability
  • Marketing analytics and digital transformation

Current research includes a longitudinal study on consumer experience with electric vehicles conducted with international collaborators.
