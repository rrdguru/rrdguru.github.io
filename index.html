The Arc of a Reflective Educator (2018–2025)

Teaching is not static. An analysis of over 700 student evaluations across three countries reveals a distinct evolutionary trajectory—moving from baseline student support toward a masterclass environment that perfectly balances intense intellectual rigor with crucial psychological safety.

Phase 3: The Transformational Facilitator IIM Ranchi (2025)
The Synthesis

Successfully merged high intellectual expectations with explicit psychological safety. Students felt secure taking intellectual risks during case discussions.

The Result

Peak datasets: 5.00/5.00 on preparedness, 4.86/5.00 overall. Students report the course changed their career intentions and gave them "hope".

Phase 2: Independence & Rigor HSE Moscow & IIM Bodhgaya (2021-24)
Pedagogical Shift

Transitioned to independent instructor teaching complex quantitative models and high-level marketing strategy. Emphasized intense academic challenge.

Feedback Incorporated

While students praised the transformational learning, some noted the environment felt overly strict. Recognized the need to actively foster a "safe space" for debate.
