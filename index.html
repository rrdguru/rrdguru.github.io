About Me

I bridge marketing theory with sustainable business practice through rigorous research and engaging pedagogy.

With a unique background combining engineering, sustainability studies, and marketing science, I investigate how product quality shapes long-term consumer behavior - particularly in durable goods and emerging technologies like electric vehicles.

My research has been published in top-tier journals including the European Journal of Marketing, and I'm currently exploring the intersection of sustainability metrics with customer-experienced product quality.
