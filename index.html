Profile

I am a marketing academic with a foundation in engineering and industry, currently serving as a Senior Assistant Professor at the newly established Faculty of Management at South Asian University (SAU), New Delhi. My academic journey is driven by a dedication to bridging the gap between rigorous quantitative research and actionable management practice.

My core research examines how product quality shapes consumer-brand relationships over time, with a forward-looking agenda emphasizing sustainable consumption. By leveraging over five years of prior industry experience in technology and manufacturing, I bring a data-driven, problem-solving perspective to both my research and my classroom.

In addition to research and teaching, I am actively engaged in institution building, student mentorship, and international academic collaborations across Europe, the Americas, and Asia.
