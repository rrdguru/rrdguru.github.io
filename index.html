Marketing, Technology and Consumer Insight

My work sits at the intersection of marketing strategy, consumer behavior and technological transformation. With a background in engineering and analytics, I examine how product quality, innovation and sustainability shape long term consumer relationships with brands.

I currently serve as founding faculty in the Faculty of Management at South Asian University, contributing to the creation of a new management education ecosystem in South Asia.

Professional Profile

Interdisciplinary Expertise
Engineering and IT industry experience combined with doctoral research in marketing allows me to integrate analytics, strategy and consumer psychology in teaching and research.
Global Teaching Experience
Teaching across Switzerland, Russia and India has exposed me to diverse student cohorts ranging from undergraduate programs to MBA and digital business programs.
Research Agenda
My research focuses on consumer experienced product quality, sustainable consumption and emerging technologies including electric vehicles and AI driven marketing analytics.

Research Direction

My research explores how consumers perceive and evaluate product quality and how these perceptions influence brand loyalty, adoption of new technologies and sustainable consumption behaviors.

  • Consumer experienced product quality
  • Electric vehicle adoption and sustainability
  • Consumer decision processes and brand relationships
  • Analytics driven marketing insights

Teaching and Pedagogy

My teaching philosophy emphasizes analytical thinking, real world application and intellectual challenge. Courses are designed to move students beyond memorization toward structured reasoning and evidence based decision making.
